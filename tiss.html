  TISSAA GENESIS

TISS has contributed immensely to our country's development by creating high-quality professionals since 1936. The alums of TISS have been providing their professional services to improve the quality of lives of people. The Alums have great potential to contribute to the mutual benefits of our institute and students.Tata Institute of Social Sciences Alums Association (TISSAA) is a registered not-for-profit organisation incorporated in 2013 under the provisions of the Bombay Public Trust Act and Societies Registration Act.

Learn More

  VISION & MISSION

The primary goal of TISSAA is to support a network of TISSians who will, in turn, help raise the profile of TISS. TISSAA not only aims to bring together like-minded TISSians close but is open to all TISS graduates and offers a broader networking scope.We are planning strategic and long-range programs to promote Alums awareness, engagement and commitment to the Institute. We want to communicate the mission and purpose of the TISS and TISSAA to the wider population.
